Title: Re: Canadian Xpress Post to the US
Post by: pharmacypowder on August 23, 2013, 01:33 pm
You WILL have to sign for an express letter coming from Canada. There is no way around this and it is one of the main reasons we do not ship to the US using express postage (but the main reason is actually for OUR own security, I am sure you all can figure out why Express envelopes leave much more of a "trail" then just regular mail.

Once your express letter hits the US and passes customs, the USPS will treat it like any other domestic express letter....It will be delivered by the express guy and you WILL have to sign for it.

Hope that helps :)

Title: Re: Canadian Xpress Post to the US
Post by: National Direct on September 10, 2013, 04:19 am
I do a lot of Canada to US shipping, and I know that things are far different when your products are legal like mine, but here's a few things people may not know:


Purolator (All Services): Do not require a signature for CA - US. This option must be purchased.

Canada Post Expedited Parcel: It's tracked, reasonably fast and no sig req'd (I think you can only do this online with a business account or at a Retail Post Office desk - No preapid version

Canada Post Tracked Packet USA: Tracked, no signature required, and I think you can pay in stamps or some other metered postage potion that only displays a generic postage rate.
