Post by: firehawk on December 09, 2012, 01:05 am
Without wanting to sound like a jerk, I would recommend you put a lot more thought into this.

Sure the glassware comes from SR, but who is the vendor? How do you know that the items haven't been identified and registered while in transit?

The safest way would be to have no-one but you know you have the glassware. How to do this? Go rob somewhere that has glassware, and of course, don't get caught.

Watch a few episodes of Breaking Bad, particularly first season. Take some notes, because he makes lots of mistakes that you would otherwise make. (On a side note, if Walter could get rid of that Jesse kid that's always landing him in trouble and sell off SR, he would be set for life).

The problem with what you're doing is it inverts the 'pyramid' of SR; the reason SR is successful is because LE don't have the resources to go after every dime-bag sold on here, and they can't trace a (good) vendor without going to serious length. You on the other hand are buying equipment for manufacture (and want it delivered at home, to boot). It's very easy for LE to either pose as a vendor of precursors or lab equipment, or pick up a single item in the post.

And then DEA and a dozen other three letter agencies will be all over you.
