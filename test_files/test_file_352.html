Title: Please help me rate this package!
Post by: oldtoby on December 07, 2012, 11:28 pm
So, I got my first package from SR and what can I say. I'm thrilled, obviously! First that I got it, second that I didn't get a love letter instead, third that there wasn't LE hiding in the bushes, etc, etc.. How ungrateful can I be, really? I am plenty fucking grateful.

That being said, I need to provide feedback and I am hesitant about providing an unqualified 5/5. No compromising details here regarding shipping. Just the following:

1) Hand-written addresses. To me this is a nonissue. It's Christmas time. I think handwritten actually seems *more* authentic than printed label, but I include this in case anyone has a view
2) Street name mis-spelled. This kind of pisses me off. I was going to say in my original order "please cut and paste address so as to avoid error" but I didn't want to sound like a tool (and, hand-written anyway, couldn't be done as it turns out). But this, I understand, is the kind of thing that gives postal carriers/inspectors pause. I'm not happy.
3) Stealth. Here I'm drawing on conversations I've followed on the forums. On the outside it's fine. Perfect. No complaints. On the inside it's dead obvious. No attempt to hide product whatsoever. Product is all there is. So what version of stealth do I go by when rating this thing? Had the package been torn, I'd be boned. Had the package been opened due to the mis-spelled address or for some other suspicion, I'd be boned. Because neither of those things happened, I was fine.

How do I rate this?
Also: I'm aware that vendors want "the chance to make things right" prior to providing anything less than a 5/5, and I'm definitely ok with doing that, but what can make this right, exactly? It's not like the package never showed up. That being said, I have ONE address to make work and no alternates. If it gets burnt due to vendor carelessness, my SR days are over.

What do you think?
