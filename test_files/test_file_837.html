I know how you feel--I've been in that horrible, paranoid mindset before and it sucks.  But the fact is--you hardly have to worry about LEOs catching up with you regarding this one order.  As has been said, if they even catch it, you'll just get a letter saying "don't do that again".  They wouldn't try to bust you unless it was possible that you were ordering with intent to distribute, and even then, there's more to it than just that.
