Title: Re: Still Having Trouble Using PGP with tormail
Post by: goldenrod on May 29, 2013, 08:52 pm
I'm using Kleopatra and it's working fine for me but we're doing completely different things with it.

When I write my msg I simply copy it (i don't paste it anywhere).
Then I rightclick the tiny kleopatra icon on the task bar.
I chose clipboard then when it gives me the next menu I chose encrypt.
It brings up another window asking me to Add recipient.
I chose the public key of the person I want to send the message to.
It reminds me to add myself as recipient and I do that as well.
Then I click okay and that part is finished.
I then rightclick into the email I want to send. Click on paste (as the encrpted msg is now on the clipboard) and it pastes the encrypted msg into the email.
At no stage do I create a file with a text extension.

Try it my way and see if it works for you...it does for me.

Good luck..I know it can be tricky at first.
