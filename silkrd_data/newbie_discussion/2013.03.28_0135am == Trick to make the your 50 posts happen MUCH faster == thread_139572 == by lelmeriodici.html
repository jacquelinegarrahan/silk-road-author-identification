Title: Trick to make the your 50 posts happen MUCH faster
Post by: lelmeriodici on March 28, 2013, 01:35 am
I was looking at my forum settings today and found some things that will help you post more quickly than I did.

Go to PROFILE > ACCOUNT SETTINGS (in the menu bar at the top of the page)
Go to MODIFY PROFILE > LOOK AND LAYOUT

Check: Return to topics after posting by default.
Check: Don't warn on new replies made while posting.
and set "Use quick reply on topic display" to "Shown, on by default"

Things should go much faster with that enabled.  Please use this thread to spam away and keep it at the top for others to see!  :)
