Hey guys, I am just curious on your thoughts about tripping solo, without a sitter. I have plenty of friends who I can get to trip with me, but i've always felt that taking a psychadelic alone can be just as fun, if not better than with your friends. Maybe this is because i am more of an introverted person. Whenever I buy shrooms or lsd, I always get enough for me to trip at least twice; once with friends, and once by myself. I think a lot of the fun of tripping with a group, is describing to each other what you are seeing/feeling. Despite all this, i still enjoy exploring my own thoughts in my room at night on a psychadelic. It is very introspective.
