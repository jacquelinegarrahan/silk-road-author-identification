SuperTrips - Where the fuck does he get all those drugs from? I mean seriously, he probably mails out a metric shitload of drugs daily. My theory is that he/his organization must manufacture them, but the idea of creating so much drugs fascinates me as well.

envious/Jannis - AL-LAD and LSZ? How the hell did they get their hands on that? I know they're sharing the same source, but where is this mystical lab manufacturing incredibly rare LSD analogues?

StExo - Very highly respected on the SR forums, and I'm very intrigued by his money laundering consultations. What does this guy do IRL?

astor - Also very highly respected on the SR forums, and I'm very curious what he does IRL to be able to give great security advice all the time.
