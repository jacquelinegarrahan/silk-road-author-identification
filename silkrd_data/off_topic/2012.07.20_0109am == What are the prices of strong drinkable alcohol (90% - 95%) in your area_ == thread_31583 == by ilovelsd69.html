I don't wanna be a smart-ass but I'd like to start this post with a warning. Don't ever drink homemade alcohol unless you or whoever made it know their shit and you're 100% sure it's pure ethanol (and water) and nothing else.
When making pure alcohol you start off by putting a special kind of yeast into a container with sugar solution. The yeast will also need vitamins and minerals and shit. For that reason you can buy bags of yeast with the nutrients mixed right in the yeast.
Then you let the container rest for a few hours/days at a good temperature and when the yeast has died, you get the alcohol out of the solution. This is where most people fuck up. They either lack both a proper still and the ability to make one, or they have a still but have no knowledge on distilling.
When distilling, you throw away the first few drops. That's because the yeast is not only making ethanol, but also methanol, ethyl acetate and other volatile esters. These are highly toxic and cause those horrible half-week hangovers mentioned by red. They can also cause blindness (the permanent kind) and death.
When I was on summer vacation in Australia my uncle, who lives there, showed me how he makes alcohol. A friend of his made him an awesome still out of an old metal beer keg. If I recall correctly, I have filmed the whole procedure with my old cell phone. I could go through all of my memory cards and share the video with you guys, if you're interested.
