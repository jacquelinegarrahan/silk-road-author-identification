Hi TNY,

There's a fee of around 30 Bitcoins ($150) to set up your seller account.

As I understand it there's also a commission on each sale :

10% of the first $50
8.5% of amounts from $50 to $150
6% of amounts from $150 to $300
3% of amounts from $300 - $500

I've never sold anything above $500 in value but I think there may be a commission for amounts above that - no doubt one of our more experienced vendors can clarify?

An important point to note is that the commission is banded therefore the commission on a sale worth $150 for example would be $13.50 (i.e 10% of the first $50 plus 8.75% of the remaining $100).

It's possible for sellers to set their prices both excluding and including sales fees.
