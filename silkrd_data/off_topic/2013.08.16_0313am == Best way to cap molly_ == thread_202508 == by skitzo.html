Title: Re: Best way to cap molly?
Post by: cantharidin on August 16, 2013, 09:16 pm
The barrel of a 1 mL plastic syringe will fit inside of a 00 cap. Cut off the end of the syringe that the needle attaches to so that you have an open barrel. Pull back the plunger to a specific volume mark and tap the end of the syringe into a pile of molly crystals until the syringe barrel is full from the plunger to the open end of the syringe. Put the open end of the syringe into the cap and press out the powder with the plunger.  Do an experiment to see what volume mark gives you the amount of weight you're looking for; you'll surprised at how reproducible this is if you take even a little bit of care (mostly in how tightly you pack the syringe). This method works great and is MUCH faster than weighing out and adjusting each dose individually.

Post by: Gozarian on August 24, 2013, 06:35 am
This is how I've done it, good with a decent amount around 5 - 6g. When I want 100mg or so per cap then I buy #3 capsules. These can typically hold 175-200mg at max capacity.

 1.  Weigh out however much you want with your scale. Easy right?

 2. Crush it up a bit so none of the chunks are larger than the opening of your capsule.

 3. Tare your scale with 1 capsule on it. I have found a #3 weighs approx .04.

 4. Open your capsule and take a paper business card and just start scooping your E into the capsule with the business card and fill 1/2 way.

 5. Quickly throw it on the scale to see if its roughly .14, if its a bit above or below its fine.

 6. Rinse and repeat.

Once you are finished count your capsules. If you have 50 and you initially weighed out 5 grams you are spot on. Give yourself a pat on the back and go have some fun.

I can typically do 5g in about 20 minutes this way.
