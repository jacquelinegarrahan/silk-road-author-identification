Title: Re: Crazy times on benzo's !
Post by: Ben on May 30, 2013, 12:33 am
If you have been taking 8 x 2 mg clonazepam a day for some time (say a month or more) it is very likely you are addicted or at least dependent.

This doesn't mean you're a junky or a lost cause or anything like that - as you mention you are still able to perform your job and all. When buying in bulk amounts i suppose the cost of the habit isn't ruining your life either - realistically in most western countries smoking a pack a day is as expensive as downing a strip of benzo's if your choice a day. Functional benzodiazepine addicts are quite normal, though most keep it to say 3 or 4 bars of xanax a day. You are however taking quite a large amount, and i'd think that would impair your functioning in some respect. The risk is that you will eventually use benzo's in situations where you should be in full control of your faculties - such as driving, but if you are a mechanic also while doing your work.

I'm not here to lecture you though. If you have been taking this amount and functioning for a long period of time, i suppose the conclusion must be that you can. Just try to keep it to as low an amount as you can, and taper off a bit if you can. Also, be careful with the alcohol. Having a beer or two is not that likely to pose extreme danger, but if you go out of a night of drinking while taking that amount of clonazepam there is a serious risk of respiratory depression. Tolerance will not prevent that completely - something you should consider if you ever consider getting seriously drunk.
