First of all Silkyyyy your advice on this thread is very very accurate and good and I would like to reiterate what most people on here have been saying by stating that you have to have the right mindset to enjoy this. It is very subjective, but being one of those people my many years of experience of this drug are awesome.

mito - it has been absolutely great reading your accounts of your first time. I have introduced a fair few of my friends into this drug.

Since having a little one I only do these things around once every 3-6 months or so and crave it somewhat a lot.

Now on to my experiences and advice:

This drug is not a social drug
This is not for everyone

Never start with a massive amount (if its your first few times), I have never seen anybody who has done this enjoy it and I have seen a lot of newcomers to this special, special dug. So dripfeed the mind, start with 10mg upward just like mito has....

A successful way I have found is to all sit down, talk your friends through nice things (a bit like them terrible mood tapes you got in the 80's/90's) while they are having small doses - helps them relax and get used to the unusual feeling of disassociation to the senses.

Music (Trippy techno, minimal, tech-house, progressive house are my weapons of choice) can be a great addition to your experience, if you are on your own then headphones and eyes closed can take you some awesome places.

An awesome experience me and my friends discovered was that getting in a really comfy seat, beanbag etc and turning the lights off and then putting on an animated movie like Over The Hedge and doing some rather large lines (200mg as a guess) would immerse you in the screen and make you feel like your in the movie. We coined it Ket Movie Nights.

Now for the not so great experiences I have noticed:

Take too much and you forget what happened which can be disappointing.
Not great in a club as too easy to take too much (good though in v small amounts!)
It feels like your standing pissing forever during a session.
Nose feels like crap.
DISGUSTING taste, especially when it drips down the back of your throat after snorting.
Feel a bit spacy a few hours after - from my experience do not play 5 a side footie in goals after a sesh as it is pretty amusing how bad you will be :p  ::)
Don't eat it, we tried to bomb it once, you need at least 500mg to feel anything, the onset is slow and the experience is so mild and not like the things we look for when snorting.
My friends have all worried about bladder problems and we can't say whether prolonged excessive use will damage your bladder but I get a feeling that it's not far off being right!
Beware - there is a shortage in the UK at the moment so people are fobbing off all sorts of stuff as K, don't just assume it's K if they say it is!

I have had lots of LSD, 2C-B and K and can honestly say K has given me the best out of this planet trips of my life. LSD was awesome but nothing too trippy more of an extreme mood enhancer for me.

Silkyyyy - I am very interested to find out more about injecting it, all I think of when saying that is heroin and stuff and makes me feel like it is something dark and nasty (as you can tell I never want to try heroin).

I know intramuscular injections would be faster and I guess, cleaner it's just taking that step and feeling safe knowing someone who has done it before can give some good guidance  ;)

Hope that helps people - I have never shared anything like that before outside my circle of friends. I am new here and like the look of these forums.

Hey mito,

Great you have been having the positive experience you have been. Ketamine is a rare and blessed substance.

If you want to get to best from it I suggest quit sorting it and start taking it IM. You can use a U-100 microfine insulin syringe with a needle as thin as a hair and a mere 15mm long - in the shoulder muscle it's simple and easy. IM has a much better energy in the body and you will find the experience much smoother.

Dosage, if you want to explore properly (which I sense you do), should be in the 100mg to 300mg range. John Lilly has explored up to the 400-500mg range, but the max I have done is 300mg and I felt I was at the outer limit of what K had to offer there. Most of my trips were at the 100mg - 150mg range, which was adequate.

Shoot it, lie down in a dimly lit room with soft music or silence, close your eyes and let it take you (Brian Eno's Apollo is quintessential K music!). Make sure you are warm (a light blanket over you is good). Once you come out, STAY DOWN for at least an hour to allow your etheric body to re-integrate with your physical body. A sitter is good.

I've only done racemic Ketamine personally, but from what I hear the S isomer is more interesting than both the R and racemic mix.

Treat K with great respect. It will bite you if you do not. It is a very deep psychedelic and it will bend reality around you. Keep it to a max of once a week - preferably 1-2 times per month. If you are casual with it you can get addicted through sheer fascination. It tends to trap the more intellectually inclined and intelligent explorers because the depth of knowledge revealed is so seductive. Be aware of this and you will be fine.

As you will have probably noticed so far, there is total ego dissolution above 100mg IM (perhaps 150mg - 200mg insufflated). If you want to crystallize some measure of identity out in the K space and be a more volitional explorer as it were, then you can use 2C-B for this purpose (normally 10-20mg). The time I did 300mg K was with 30mg 2C-B, and was a very heavy trip - but worth it! (Take the 2C-B an hour to 90 minutes before the K.)

Continue to treat it with respect and it will reward you with the deepest insights that psychedelics have to offer. But know this: K is not the way anywhere. It is a closed-loop Mobius and does not lead to transcendence (though it may help catalyze it).
