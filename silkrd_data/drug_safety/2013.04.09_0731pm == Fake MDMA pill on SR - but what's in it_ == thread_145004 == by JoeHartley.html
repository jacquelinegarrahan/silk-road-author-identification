Title: Fake MDMA pill on SR - but what's in it?
Post by: JoeHartley on April 09, 2013, 07:31 pm
Ok, so I ordered ten "MDMA" pills from a new yet reputable SR MDMA vendor, but these pills definitely didn't contain any MDMA. Upon examining the pills I noted they were a little chalky in both consistency and taste. They didn't smell like MDMA, and I'm 100% positive as I had some crystals on hand at the time. The chemical tasted bitter; similar to MDMA but definitely not in that family. I decided to try one of the two smaller pills out, as I was pretty sure the vendor wouldn't send me something seriously harmful.

Out of the ten pills, six were yellow and flat with an 'M' similar to the McDonald's logo printed on one side and a straight groove down the other side. They measured 8mm in diameter and 5mm in height. There was one blue pill with the mitsubishi logo printed on one side, and a straight groove down the other, and it had exactly the same measurements as the yellow pills. There was an orange/pink pill which was slightly smaller than the yellow ones and it had one rounded side. There were also two smaller 'M' printed pills; one green and one purple. These were the ones I decided to try out, as they were the smallest.

15 minutes after taking one pill I began to feel it kicking in. This confirmed to me that these pills didn't contain MDMA, as I always take at least an hour to start a buzz off that. By 45 minutes I was pretty stimulated and I decided it was time to go out. My body was flushing hot and my skin was tingling, but not in the same magical way as with MDMA. Don't get me wrong, I felt pretty damn good; I was euphoric and running my hands and face under cool water was amazing. I later realised that I was pretty much peaking. My vision and physical functions were completely fine so I decided to go for a drive. Driving felt really great for a bit, but by 1hr 30 minutes after taking the pill I was definitely not as high as I previously was.

At the 2 and a half hour mark I decided to take the other small pill, but in two halves with a 30 minute interval between them as I was still unsure of exactly what the substance was. The re-dose provided a little more stimulation, but hardly any euphoria. I guess it's kind of a one-shot dose deal with this drug. The comedown was fine, and I did some coke about an hour and a half after taking the second half of the second pill. The coke felt good, and after a short while I was hardly feeling any effects from the pills. I smoked some weed and went to sleep, and the next day I didn't really have a hangover.

Overall this drug was fun, although a little underwhelming with its duration. Maybe higher doses are the trick, but before I do that I'm going to message the vendor that sold me these "MDMA" pills and see what they say about my problem. Hopefully they are cooperative and we will be able to work something out. I also want to make sure that they're not selling these pills as MDMA any more.

I just want some fucking MDMA pills after all…is that too much to ask?!

To the community out there, what do you think was in these pills? I've narrowed it down to mephedrone (4-methylmethcathinone [4-MMC] ) or methylone (bk-MDMA) due to the effects the substance had on me. I personally think it's mephedrone as it didn't last as long as methylone supposedly does, and it had slightly different characteristics based off trip reports that I've read.

I appreciate any comments that anyone might like to give ;)
- JH

Set & setting has a HUGE part of the MDMA experience. If you are not in a good mood when you take it, or you are anxious it will be different.
I've taken the exact same MDMA on 7 - 10 different occassions and I noticed that each was different. If I am with large groups of people, it will be better.
if I'm worried, scared or anxious at all, it will make t he MDMA feel weaker.

Also, 15 minutes is plenty of time for MDMA to kick in. Usually for me it's about 30 minutes, but it's been as low at 10 - 15 mins and also up to 1 hour 45 minutes (I thought they were duds after about an hour, but was pleasantly surprised when it kicked in).

Also, have you taken MDMA recently? Because any MDMA use in the last 2-3 months will affect it. And heavy usage from the past few years will gradually weaken the experience as well.
