Title: Re: Favorite Bumping/Euphoric/Casual Feelgood Drug?
Post by: MariaSabina on August 03, 2013, 11:01 am
Quote from: chil on August 03, 2013, 10:12 am
Quote from: tommygun on August 03, 2013, 03:08 am
Do people ever freak out on ketamine, is it a panic prone drug?

It's likely that Ket's dissociative effect might get some people very anxious...not for everyone.

It's psychedelic, right, so the space a person is in could turn towards fear (along a continuum of "rationality"), but it could at the same time (paradoxically) create a space where that feeling/idea can really be examined and seen for what it really is, so then the problem resolves itself.

If someone is really prone to being very anxious and still really wants to try a bump of K and finds herself being overwhelmed with anxiety during the experience and doesn't want to deal with that anxiety, then having like 2.5-5mg of Valium on hand might be helpful for her in case things get too scary.  Erowid says not to mix the two!  But, my personal experience is that the Valium can take the "edge off".  There is a study (PubMed ID of PMC2844443) that says "a ketamineâ€“diazepam combination might be a clinically useful therapeutic option for the treatment of refractory SE" where SE is referring to a prolonged seizure, so it sounds like the combo could be medically useful for someone in an emergency, and that makes me less worried about the combo.  Also, keeping the amounts consumed of both on the smaller end would obviously be safer.  Anyway, be careful, your mileage may vary, etc.  :)
