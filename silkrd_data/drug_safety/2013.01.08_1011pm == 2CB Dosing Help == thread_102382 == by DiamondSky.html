Title: Re: 2CB Dosing Help
Post by: stinkybreeze on January 09, 2013, 06:30 am

Quit screwing around and get an .001 scale. The ones on amazon for like $20-$25 bucks work fine. They come with weights to recalibrate and such. That's what I have. I had the .001-10grams scale, then I bought the .001-20grams scale as well. Same company.

Scales are most accurate in the mid range of the max and minimum. So when I am dealing with 2cb doses what I do is place a piece of paper on the scale that is a few grams (or piece of cardboard), then push the tare button (zero it out). Then you tap out 10, 15, 20mg, etc. Put in a gelcap for later (can get a health food store).

When measuring such small doses you have to make sure there is no moving air in the room---fan or heat/air cond. Fluctuations in the air make the scale go up and down wildly. Also don't breathe on the scale or you will see it move as well.

Do this and you'll be good.
