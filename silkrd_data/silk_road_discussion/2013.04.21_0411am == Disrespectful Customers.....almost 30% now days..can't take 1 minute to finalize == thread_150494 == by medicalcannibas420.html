There is actually one instance where I would prefer that customers not finalize right away and I learned this the hard way. When the BTC is in freefall, even if the orders are hedged, once they finalize and it's in your SR wallet and the BTC is going down, down, down, you will see your wallet drop accordingly.

I had a ton of orders finalize when BTC was at ~$250 and lost a ton of money. If only those customers had waited a day or two till the BTC dropped to like $70, I still would have been paid the BTC equivalent of my USD and wouldn't have lost any money at all...

So a word of advice for the good customers out there, if you see BTC in freefall you might want to message your vendor and ask if they would prefer you wait a few days before finalizing to see if the BTC stabilizes.

Just my $.02.
