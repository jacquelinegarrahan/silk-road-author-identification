SNEAKY BANDITS! [FORUM ONLY VENDORS]

Don't get harvested, only do business with legit vendors! Lots of exclamations to emphasize the import of the message here! Security is Priority #1!

STICKY WIKI [FORUM CLEANUP]

The Wiki is going to be updated to be more comprehensive and helpful to users as part of an initiative to clean up the forum to make it pretty and easier to navigate and more balanced for all of you. The Rumor Mill in particular had a large number of stickies, making it difficult to access the individual vendor threads. These have been linked to in a single sticky now, so that the page is primarily floating (not sticky) threads. Same deal in the Security subforum.

-------------------------------------------

MT GIX, I4DOLLAR, BIT.LY, BTKOIN [SCAMMER]

There has been a scammer making numerous accounts and posting on the forum with this thread title:

Regardless of which domain/name they happen to be using, please do NOT give these people your money. No legitimate business operates in this manner.
