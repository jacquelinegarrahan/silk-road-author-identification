2c-e is fun and pretty visually intense, a little tweaky but not bad.
2c-i (the reason I initially joined) is more energetic and while more fun (and emotional) the visuals leave something to be desired.
2c-p is a LONG one, slow comeup but fun, last a good 12 hours but it's a slow burn. However you'll probably feel very hot and look pretty shitty (sweaty, tired, etc...) after about the 4 hour mark. However, at 8mg, it's basically like being sober but with visuals.

All 2c-x chems tend to leave me feeling exausted when they wear off, I also usually feel a bit drained for the next day.

LSD  is nice, and easy going, it can be mentally confusing but for the most part, if you go slow and work your self up (take 50mcg more each trip) you'll learn how to handle it.

25i, and 25c are both HORRIBLE ideas, they fuck with your blood pressure pretty badly and can seriously hurt you. Not to mention that they can mess up your ability to trip on anything else in the future.
(I've only had 25c twice and I still regret it, both times)

shrooms can be fun, terrifying, or very soul searchy and there is no real way to know what your going to get out of it each time. ...for me at least.
4-ho-met was fun but a little expensive and kind of odd, I got headaches and other weirdness, not to mention the anxiety got to me with this stuff. Stick to real shrooms.
