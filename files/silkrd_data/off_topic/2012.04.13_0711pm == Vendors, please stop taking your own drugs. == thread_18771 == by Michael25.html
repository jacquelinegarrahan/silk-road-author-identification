In my opinion, it's definitely not worth $22 a pill. I think 20mg of Adderall is worth about $5-10 or so. Obviously people are willing to pay much more than that and SR fees/convenience means it's reasonable for vendors to charge more, but I'm just saying for me, personally, it's only maybe $.50/mg worth of fun and usefulness, all things considered.
