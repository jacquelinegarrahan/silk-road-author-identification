I haven't purchased from 'em myself, but Tessellated is apparently *the* vendor for acid.

Possessing small amounts of LSD won't land you in too much trouble; and they'll only test you for it if they think you're vendoring large amounts as the test costs roughly $8k. How harsh law enforcement will be on you generally varies from state to state (presuming you're in the US); if you're in a red state I'd be careful.

As for adulteration- LSD isn't adulterated so much as people will sell you something else claiming it's LSD, usually 25i-NBOMe. Regardless, most psychedelics aren't neurotoxic or liver damaging so there's not too much to fear. You can tell if it's real based on length, and some subjective effects- a feeling of energy in the body, words and shadows dancing, intense closed-eye visuals, and typically your mind will delve into scientific or philosophical quandries like the concept of ∞, quantum states, space exploration, how every piece of your body is condensed particle vibrations; how you really feel about life, wether or not your situation is as bad or good as you think, and for many people the subject of death- you can become quite content with the thought that you'll die some day, without fearing it. However, not only do acid trips vary from person to person- they vary each time you take it, depending on mood and what you've been recently thinking about.
