Title: Re: LSD dosage first time user
Post by: dss3i on October 31, 2012, 05:52 am
Quote from: yzxcv12693 on October 30, 2012, 07:05 pm
Hmm, what are the negative experiences I can make if I OD?

You might find it uncomfortable if you forget how to move, talk etc during your trip, but I see that as something you have to sacrifice for insights. If you find it uncomfortable loosing your basic functions, such as not being able to communicate with anyone, you might have a panic attack. Also, higher doses may bring back old unpleasant memories or force you to face unpleasant truths about your life. If your thoughts and feelings really bother you you can change the music, try some food, or go to another room, and you might start thinking about something else, something more pleasant. Having a lot of people around can also be scary while on acid, so you want to be in a safe place and have a bed at hand where you can be alone, without a lot of people running around. Finding it hard to eat is another negative effect while on acid, so eat well before taking the acid. The trick when becoming uncomfortable on acid is to stay calm and remember that it will pass, and you should be okay. I think that most 'bad' trips on acid are 'unplanned' trips, where people are offered acid at a party and try it without knowing what to expect, and they freak out when they realize they can't talk/move/eat, and a lot of unfamiliar distorted faces are partying all around them. If you feel that you really can't handle a trip a last way out is to take some benzos, because they work as an antidote. It might give you a sense of security to keep some laying around.

Personally, yes, I think it is a bad idea to take LSD for the first time without a sober sitter. When you are experienced you may know which dose you can handle on your own, but the first time it is important to know someone will help you if you aren't feeling well.

If you do take with your two friends and you only take 120 mics my guess is that you will be able to take a walk, and that you will be able to even if you take 200 mics, because at least one of you will probably be functioning well enough to guide the walk. My experience is that you can forget how cold it really is when on LSD, so remember to wear warm clothes. There is also a possibility that you will decide to do something stupid and dangerous while on LSD. I wouldn't say the risk is high, but it definitely exists.

At least tell someone sober that you will be taking LSD so that you can call this person in case of an emergency.
