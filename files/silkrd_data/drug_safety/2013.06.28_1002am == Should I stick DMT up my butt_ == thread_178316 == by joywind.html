Either way, I realized the experiment was not over YET. :)
So I wiped my ass (which was feeling considerably better after) and laid back down on my bed.
The DMT comeup feeling continued to grow for about 2-3 minutes.

I could almost feel the DMT moving through my veins and into my brain like small waves on the beach as the tide is going up. That steady, rhythmic increasing. End Report

DMT freebase won't dissolve in water that easy. The fumarate salt of DMT on the other hand, does dissolve very good. There are a few teks out there that describe the extraction of DMT straight to the salt.

IIRC, in order to use other administration routes for DMT, one needs a MAOi. DMT is being metabolized very fast and slow onset doesn't work. Smoking, IV or IM are the routes without a MAOi.

For oral, rectal and all other sorts of administration, a MAOi is required to prevent DMT being attacked by this enzyme. The MAOi will inhibit the function of the enzyme so it can't metabolize DMT.
