I was so scared to try DMT when I first extracted it myself.  Two things have made my experiences amazing and I think are a must.

DMT is not an everyday thing.  It is however, an amazing, euphoric, SPIRITUAL journey that leaves you feeling at such peace its incredible. 

I've found MXE to help with the pre-flight jitters, its pretty good for that, just be careful not to do it at the end of an mxe binge, the extra dopamine/stimulating push can increase chances for panic/paranoia.  I've had bad DMT experiences, but they were when I didn't respect the drug and smoked too much with the wrong drugs in my system. 

Mixing changa with MDMA was a bad call for me, it's debated at the dmt-nexus whether it really does have any inherent risk but I experienced either serotonin syndrome or a panic attack, either way, not fun. 

Respect DMT and she will treat you well.  Buy a vaporizer, it's worth it if you want to travel in hyperspace.
