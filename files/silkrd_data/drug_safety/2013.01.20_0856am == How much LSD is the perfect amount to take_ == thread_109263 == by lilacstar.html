Title: How much LSD is the perfect amount to take?
Post by: lilacstar on January 20, 2013, 08:56 am
I'm wondering what dose you guys take when you want to trip super hard, but don't want to be completely gone.

Also what is the most you ever took, and what was that experience like?

I'm asking because the only doses I ever took is 100ug and 250ug at different times. The 100ug I didn't get any visuals but it was still awesome with the whole mind part. The 250ug I got fractals and patterns moving and everything waving and awesome closed eye visuals as well as brighter colors and very mild shadow hallucinations. But I wasn't tripping a whole lot, as I could still have normal conversations with people and they wouldn't notice I was on anything unless they looked at my saucer pupils.

I would love to double, or even triple my highest normal dose to get insane visuals and hallucinations. But at the same time, I don't want to take too much that I'd end up thinking I'm "a glass of orange juice". I still want to be safe but trip hard.

What is the perfect dose for you guys?
Title: Re: How much LSD is the perfect amount to take?
Post by: MaidMandy on January 20, 2013, 09:14 am
The effects of LSD are very subjective and it really hits people in different ways. For me, I would be absolutely tripping balls on somewhere between 120ug - 150ug. At that dose, I would find it very hard to hide that I was twisted on some drug or other. Acid heads would know a mile away what I was up to :)

The biggest dose I have taken was about 300ug and I was properly hallucinating. It was an incredible experience - I spent about 3 hours meditating during the trip and I felt like I had been reborn after I came down from the trip. I don't really want to start taking higher doses. Simple reason being that I don't want LSD to lose it s magic for me and I don't want to build a crazy high tolerance, like I have done with MDMA. I keep my trips few and far between and I always have a blast.

In your case, you should try pushing the boundaries with higher doses if that's what you feel is right. Just remember, there is only so high you can go and if you keep taking higher doses to get to a higher place, it could back fire with incredibly high tolerance...that would suck, in my opinion as the magic would be lost.

All the best,

Maid Mandy

Title: Re: How much LSD is the perfect amount to take?
Post by: Wazup7 on January 21, 2013, 09:24 pm
I usually take 1 hit, whatever dosage that may be from the batch I get it from.  I have been getting pretty consistently dosed papers these past couple years, between 80-120ug per tab.  They have been vouched for enough times by enough highly-experienced users that I trust the findings without actually going and getting the papers tested myself.  (When I first was introduced to L, I made some friends who were really heavy in the 60s psychedelic scene, and never got out of it, so I look to them for advice I can trust--I gotta think that tripping every few months for 50 years, and never losing a connection to L--that they can judge potency pretty accurately.  I mean I've been using LSD for 10 years and I think I have a pretty good grasp of dosages, assuming that my conservative baseline estimates are about correct)  But I'm not going to get any further into that point--that's a whole different topic

So for me, 80ug is usually enough to party.  150ug (~2 hits) is enough to get me tripping out.  240ug is enough to get me melting and swirling.  And then there are the monster trips...my first monster I ever took was 7 hits of ~100ug tabs, which sent me into space for a good 48 hours.  I'm talking about swimming in the clouds, laughing at grass, licking the wind, and touching anything and everything because it just feels so nice!

I've only tripped like that twice--once with paper, and another time with freshly mixed liquid.  both trips were about the same dosage.  I absolutely loved the high dose trips, but that's probably because my set and setting were completely optimal (weekend long music festivals on a private farm outside of a small, rural, Pennsylvania town, with close friends, female companionship, good music of all different kinds, and a high degree of freedom from law enforcement (cops were there, but they were only concerned with keeping the peace, not with busting people for using or selling drugs).  I have wanted to take another monster trip for a long time, but I simply haven't been in another situation where the set and setting were so perfect for doing that. 

So yeah, for me, 1/2/3 hits are my typical domain of dosages, and of all the times I take 1/2/3 hits, I probably take 1 hit like 90% of the time.  Once you know how LSD works, you don't need a monster dose to get a monster trip.  I'm pretty trained at this point to make whatever I want to of my trip, as long as I have some minimum amount of ug in me, and the hits I've been getting lately have been good enough for just 1 hit to get me there.

Enjoy!

Title: Re: How much LSD is the perfect amount to take?
Post by: The Scientist on January 26, 2013, 05:47 am
200ug to 400ug is the ideal amount if want to experience the full spectrum of psychedelic effects, up to and including a spiritual experience (by which I mean so-called "cosmic consciousness" or Satori-Samadhi levels of consciousness - a sense of complete unity with everything in the universe, which is the highest state of bliss).

100ug can sometimes evoke a spiritual experience, but it will usually only last momentarily at the peak of the trip. At 200-400ug, it can last almost the entire duration of the trip, and it is more intense as well.

If I take anything less than 200ug, I feel I haven't taken enough.

200-400ug was also the dosage used in LSD psychotherapy in the 1960s.
