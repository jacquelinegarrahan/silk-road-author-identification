Title: Tasteless Blotters as LSD (bought from Lucydrop), What else could it be?
Post by: MoonlightInParis on March 19, 2013, 06:50 am
I bought some LSD blotters from Lucydrop in late February. I tried them with a friend of mine. The blotters were like thin cardboard and were tasteless. We took a tab each and as we didn't feel anything after 1 hour, we took the second dose. The effects started to come up after 2-3 hours. There was a mild nausea, a lot of muscle aching in the back and neck, and an uncomfortable body load. We had some laughing burts. My friend was saying that he felt cold and both of us had occasional goosebumps. There was a mild pupil dilation. There were very slight OEVs (only if you tried hard!), but no vibrant colors. Rather, there was this feeling of anxiety and a bad feeling in my gut. I tried to close my eyes but the CEVs were so gloomy, dark, unpleasant, and void of any colors. By this time, both of us had come to believe that the tabs did not contain clean LSD (or no LSD at all). After 4-5 hours, despite of being completely sober, both of us were feeling really uncomfortable as there was this sensation of terrible anxiety and agitation that was getting worse and worse by the time. It came to the point that both of us had to take some Lorazepam to calm the terrible anxiety. My friend was complaining of a tremor in his hands. The anxiety got better after taking the Lorazepam tabs, however I could still feel the remaining unpleasant body load and the bad feeling in my gut for probably 18 hours after taking the tabs. I even wouldn't call it a trip!, it was one of the worst experiences we had ever had.
I strongly believe that the blotters didn't contain real LSD. I noticed some other costumer of LD had complained of a similar experience with Lucy's tabs on the forums.
Do you think it was weak LSD tainted with other drugs, or a completely different drug (RC)?
Given the tabs (card board like) were tasteless, what could be the content of the tabs?
