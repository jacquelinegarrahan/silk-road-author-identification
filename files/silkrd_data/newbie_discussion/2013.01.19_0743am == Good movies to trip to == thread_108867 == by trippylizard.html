Post by: signor on January 22, 2013, 10:44 am
The very first time I tried acid, I was jamming on some music at a friend's place.  I had no idea how long an acid trip would take to kick in, nor how long it'd last for.  The end result was that when it came time to go back home I was still tripping my face off.  I was only 19 and lived with my parents still.. and I was pretty keen not to hang around them too much, as I was paranoid that it'd be completely fkn obvious that I was tripping if I said two words.  So I went straight to my room and turned on the TV and saw a film called "Breaking The Rules".. and it was the BEST thing I'd ever seen.  Fucking insane.. intense.. hilarious.. clever.. beautifully shot.. just perfect.  Of course at some point I had to go to the toilet.. on my way back I got distracted by the newspaper or something and totally forgot about the film.

but then the next day, totally overcome by how awesome my whole afternoon/evening had been.. and going through my day's obligations in a total daze.. I was still overwhelmed with what an awesome movie I'd seen.  And I was annoyed that I'd missed half of it.  So that evening, on my way home, I went and hired it on VHS.  I couldn't wait another day to see it.  Turns out the film was a pile of rubbish.

this whole story is really just an exercise in wasting all your time of course.. I could have just said that if you're tripping hard enough and you have a good mindset about it, pretty much ANYTHING can seem amazing.

On a slightly diff note, whenever I'm around anyone else who's tripping super hard I always like to put on some ZZ Top vids - either something from Live In Texas, or any of the MTV era clips.  It always gets to people.. partly because they're never expecting it.. partly because the visuals are so tripper-friendly.. partly because the music is just that little bit warped and off-beat.
